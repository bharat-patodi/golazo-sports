Team

Enrico Manganaro

UEFA-B Licensed coach, and Head Coach at Golazo Football Academy (GFA), Enrico, is committed to share knowledge and hands-on training to improve grassroots football in India. A renowned coach and tactical expert, Enrico has a vast amount of experience in mentoring kids internationally namely, in France, Italy and Portugal. A former skilled footballer in Italy, Enrico has successfully experienced all the steps in the Youth football coaching pyramid, from U-11 to U-19.

Surja Yadav

Hailing from the Mecca of Indian football; Kolkata, our home-bred football Guru, Surja is a AFC-C License holder. He has also held advisory roles for the Indian national team at various levels.

Steven Fernandes

Having played CBSE football nationals, University football in Delhi, and then eventually First Division football with New Delhi Heroes F.C., Steven is a true-blue football enthusiast and entrepreneur. He adds technical expertise to the team with his unbound experience in the Indian football industry. He is a Manchester United supporter.

Divyank Sood

Co-founder at Golazo Sports, Divyank has a wide network within the football industry both, India and abroad. Having done his MBA and further studies in the US, he hold a good amount of experience with the top firms in New York. A footballer at heart, he is an Arsenal fan.

Aditya Choudhary

Founding member at Golazo Sports, Aditya, after graduating as an I.T. engineer, went on to develop his entrepreneurial skills in his various successful ventures. He developed a wide-array of connections with football academies and clubs during his time in UK. He is an avid Liverpool supporter.
